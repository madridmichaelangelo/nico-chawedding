How It All Began

Our Story

✦

From a chance meeting to a promise of forever, here are some quick but memorable moments that brought us to this day.

October 2017

The First Hello

We first met during our Mathematics Wizards Society's General Assembly. At the time, I only knew Cha as the girl who captivated everyone with her dance presentation. After the assembly, we became friends, and as the days passed, my admiration for her grew deeper.

July 8, 2021

A Promise Made

There were a few attempts at courtship along the way, but our love story needed more time to unfold. In December 2020, I officially courted her with sincere intentions — and after months of patience, prayers, and perseverance, she said yes on my birthday, the most unforgettable gift I could ever receive.

August 2, 2025

A Promise of Forever

At the beautiful Riverfront Garden Resort in Maragondon, Cavite, surrounded by the serenity of the moment and years of memories we'd built together, I asked the love of my life to marry me. She said yes once again.
